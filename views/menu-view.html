How to play

Classic Mode
  • There is no time limit.Take your time to accomplish your target.
  • There are 4 levels. There are several floating bowls in each level.
  • First level is started from sea level.
  • There are 3 pearls to be collected.
  • You need to reach level 4 to collect pearls.
  • Once you reach level 4 with at least one pearl then you won.
  • If you loose all the pearls before reaching level 4 then you loose.
  • You will get coins for collecting pearl in the bowl in each level.
  • In Hard mode, you'll get more coins.
  • In Easy mode, you'll get less coins.
  • There are free lives depending on difficulties.
  • There are 6 free lives in Easy mode.
  • There are 3 free lives in Hard mode.
  • If you fail to collect pearl in bowl then you can use life to get back lost pearl.
  • You'll earn coins on each level upgradation depending on your difficulty level.
Classic Mode
  • There is 3 min time limit. You need to earn coins with collecting pearls as much as you can.
  • Other rules are same as above.
